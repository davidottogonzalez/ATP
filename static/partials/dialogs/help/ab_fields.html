This is the lists of fields from the data set. While building attributes, these fields are what specifies what the query will return. For example, the field F004 determines the zip code and can be used to build an attribute that specifies a certain region.
There are two ways to add the field to the attribute's expression. First, you can click on the desired field and then click the 'Add' button; this will place the selected field to the first available box. The second is to drag the desired field and then drop it into the desired box. You can also remove fields from the expression by clicking the remove button; this will remove the right most field.
